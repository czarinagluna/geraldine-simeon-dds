Meet Dr. Simeon

Dr. Geraldine Simeon is a trusted primary care dental provider for patients throughout Brooklyn Heights and the New York City area. At Geraldine F. Simeon DDS PC, she offers comprehensive dental care services including exam, oral cancer screening, x-rays, and cleanings, as well as restorative services which include fillings and crowns.

Dr. Simeon graduated from the New York University College of Dentistry in 1999 and has 20 years of experience in the field of dentistry. Prior to opening her private practice in 2003, she was an associate practicing general dentistry. Currently she is also a Senior Associate Pediatric and Adult Dentist at Melville Kids Dentistry in Melville, Long Island, NY for five years now.

Committed to helping patients maintain and improve their oral health, Dr. Simeon provides the highest quality and level of care in a personalized setting, and builds relationships for life with her patients and families. She promotes oral health and oral disease prevention through oral hygiene instruction, education to patients, and preventative treatment options.

Dr. Simeon welcomes you to her dental office Geraldine F. Simeon DDS PC! We would love to hear from you at our direct telephone line to set your appointment. All dental visits in our office are made through reservations, and our office accepts dental PPO insurance plans.
